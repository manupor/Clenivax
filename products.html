Features and Benefits

Antimicrobial Action

CHG is a broad-spectrum antiseptic that targets various bacteria, fungi, and some viruses, ensuring comprehensive skin disinfection.

Long-lasting Effects

The antimicrobial activity of CHG continues to work for hours after application, providing prolonged protection against microbial contamination.

User-friendly Design

The applicators are designed for ease of use, allowing healthcare professionals to apply the solution evenly and efficiently.

Versatile Applications

Besides preoperative skin preparation, CHG products are also used in wound care and for cleansing around central venous and urinary catheters.

Reduced SSI Risk

Regular use of CHG applicators has been shown to lower the incidence of surgical site infections, contributing to better patient outcomes.

Effective Against Resistant Bacteria

Effective against MRSA, VRE, CRE, Clostridium difficile, and most viruses and fungi.

Product Precautions

  1. Use with caution for infants under 2 months of age and premature infants. The product may cause irritation or burns to baby skin.
  2. This product is disposable, please discard it immediately after use.
  3. Sponge is latex free and externally applied.
  4. Use in a well ventilated area.
  5. Do not use it for lumbar puncture or meningeal surgery.
  6. Do not use it for open wounds or for daily cleaning of skin.
  7. Do not use it in patients allergic to chlorhexidine gluconate or isopropanol.
  8. Do not touch eyes, ears or mouth.
  9. Store at 15-30 °C (58-86 °F).
  10. Avoid freezing or storing at temperatures above 40 °C (104 °F) to maintain optimal efficacy.
  11. Before using chlorhexidine gluconate disinfectant applicator, please carefully read the instructions on the package and follow the doctor's advice.
