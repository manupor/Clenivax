AORN Guidelines Compliance

2025 Standards

Clenivax™ is fully compliant with the AORN Guidelines for Perioperative Practice (2025), Guideline for Preoperative Patient Skin Antisepsis.

Key Compliance Points

  • Recommended Agent: CHG 2% with Isopropyl Alcohol 70% — designated preferred preoperative skin antiseptic for intact skin.
  • Persistent Activity: CHG provides residual antimicrobial effect lasting >6 hours post-application, exceeding AORN minimum requirements.
  • Application Technique: Clenivax™ applicator design supports AORN-recommended two-step, back-and-forth friction technique for full-contact coverage.
  • Drying Time: Allows full evaporation of isopropyl alcohol before draping, consistent with AORN fire-safety protocols (minimum 3 minutes).
  • Sterile Field Protection: Single-use, sterile applicator eliminates risk of cross-contamination in the perioperative field.

Referenced Standards

  • AORN Guideline for Preoperative Patient Skin Antisepsis (2025 Edition)
  • AORN Guideline for Prevention of Retained Surgical Items
  • CDC Guideline for Prevention of Surgical Site Infection (2017)

FDA Clearance & Sterility

510(k) Cleared

Regulatory Classification

  • Device Class: Class II — Surgical Skin Antiseptic
  • Product Code: KZG / OTC Topical Antiseptic
  • Regulation Number: 21 CFR 333.470
  • Establishment Registration: FDA-registered manufacturing facility

Formulation & Sterility

  • Active Ingredients: Chlorhexidine Gluconate 2% w/v + Isopropyl Alcohol 70% v/v
  • Sterilization Method: Ethylene Oxide (EO) — ISO 11135 validated

SSI Prevention Protocol

Evidence-Based

Surgical Site Infections (SSIs) affect approximately 2–5% of patients undergoing inpatient surgery. Clenivax™ is engineered as the evidence-based choice within multi-modal SSI prevention bundles.

Clinical Evidence

  • CHG vs. Povidone-Iodine: Meta-analysis of 13 RCTs (n=2,623) demonstrates CHG-alcohol reduces SSI rates by up to 41% compared to povidone-iodine scrub/paint (Darouiche et al., NEJM 2010).
  • Persistent Bactericidal Activity: Residual CHG activity suppresses gram-positive and gram-negative organisms for >6 hours, critical for procedure duration protection.
  • Broad Spectrum Coverage: Effective against MRSA, VRE, Candida spp., and common surgical pathogens per EUCAST/CLSI breakpoints.

Protocol Integration

  • SCIP (Surgical Care Improvement Project): Clenivax™ supports Inf-6 measure — appropriate hair removal and skin prep.
  • WHO SSI Bundle (2016): Aligned with WHO recommendation for alcohol-based antiseptic on clean-contaminated and clean wounds.
  • The Joint Commission: Supports NPSG.07.05.01 — hand hygiene and SSI prevention measures.
  • HICPAC/CDC (2017): Supports Category IA recommendation for alcohol-based antiseptic preparation.

Supported Procedure Types

  • Orthopedic implant procedures · Cardiac surgery · Neurosurgery
  • General abdominal procedures · Laparoscopic surgery · Vascular access
