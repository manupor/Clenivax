3. Protocol Integration

National Quality Programs

  • SCIP INF-6: Clenivax™ supports appropriate hair removal and skin antisepsis metrics.
  • Joint Commission NPSG.07.05.01: Aligns with requirement to implement evidence-based guidelines to prevent SSIs.
  • Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS): Incorporation into Hospital-Acquired Condition Reduction Program to mitigate penalty risk.

WHO SSI 2016 Bundle Elements

  • Preoperative bathing with antiseptic soap.
  • Alcohol-based skin preparation using CHG/IPA for intact skin.
  • Antimicrobial prophylaxis timing (within 60 minutes before incision).
  • Normothermia and glycemic control.
