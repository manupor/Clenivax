Training & Support Resources – Clenivax™ Clinical Enablement

Operational toolkit for implementing Clenivax™ skin antisepsis across perioperative teams, including education pathways, competency validation, and 24/7 clinical support infrastructure.

1. Education Pathways

Digital Learning

  • Interactive eLearning modules (SCORM 1.2 compatible) for OR nurses, anesthesia techs, and surgical technologists.
  • Self-paced microlearning playlists (5–7 minutes) covering applicator sizes and indication-specific workflows.
  • Knowledge checks with auto-graded assessments; pass mark ≥ 85%.

Instructor-Led Training

  • On-site in-service by Clenivax™ Clinical Specialists (minimum 1.5 hours, includes hands-on practicum).
  • Virtual instructor-led refresher (Zoom/MS Teams) — 45 minutes with live Q&A.
  • Competency sign-off forms preloaded into hospital LMS or printable PDF.

Certification & CE Credits

  • ANCC-approved continuing education module awarding 1.0 contact hour.
  • AST-aligned credit for surgical technologists upon completion of skills check and assessment.
  • Quarterly update webinars featuring latest SSI prevention evidence.

2. Competency Validation

Competency DomainEvaluation CriteriaEvidence Required
Product Knowledge Identifies all applicator sizes, indications, and contraindications. Score ≥ 90% on knowledge assessment, signed by educator.
Technique Execution Demonstrates friction scrub pattern, coverage boundaries, and timed drying. Skills checklist initialed by preceptor; video submission optional.
Documentation & Compliance Completes EMR documentation template accurately, including lot # and drying verification. Chart audit (3 cases) with zero documentation omissions.
Competency forms available in editable PDF (FORM-CLX-COMP-2026) and Epic/Cerner template files.

3. Resource Library

  • High-definition procedure videos (orthopedic, cardiac, neuro) with step-by-step overlays.
  • Printable quick reference cards for OR suites (laminated, wipeable).
  • Patient-facing preoperative bathing instruction sheet (English/Spanish).
  • Fire safety checklist with drying time log.
  • FAQs addressing CHG sensitivities, pediatric use, and latex concerns.

4. Support Infrastructure

Clinical Hotline

  • 24/7 nurse-led hotline: +1 (800) 555-0199 option 3.
  • Average response time < 5 minutes.
  • Escalation to Medical Science Liaison within 1 hour for complex inquiries.

Account Services

  • Dedicated strategic account manager for formulary and contracting.
  • Automated consignment/reorder monitoring with PAR level alerts.
  • Quarterly utilization reports sent to materials management.

Implementation Team

  • Project plan template (6-week go-live) with milestones and RACI matrix.
  • Shadowing program for super users and OR champions.
  • Post-implementation review at day 30 and day 90 to review KPIs.

5. Documentation Package

  • eIFU portal access (DOC-CLX-EIFU-2026) with revision alerts.
  • SDS/OSHA HazCom compliant Safety Data Sheets for all formulations.
  • Policy templates for perioperative services and infection prevention committees.
